The darts scoring app for iOS.

Dart Meister makes scoring darts effortless — so you can focus on your game.

Whether you're a beginner learning the rules or a seasoned player tracking every throw, Dart Meister's intuitive throw input system guides you through each round. See valid throws at a glance, visualize calculations, and let the app handle the math. Upgrade to Pro for personal statistics and full game history.

Supports Cricket, x01 & Shanghai with up to 4 players or teams.
